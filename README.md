### Game addons — because I solve problems wherever I find them 🎮

I play WoW and ESO and I write addons when the game doesn't do what I need it to.

**[AutoSellPlus](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/autosellplus)** · World of Warcraft · Lua  
Automatically sells junk, low item level greens and blues when you visit a vendor — with transmog protection so you don't accidentally vendor that rare appearance you've been farming for. Smart filters, configurable item level thresholds, and one-click confirmations. Born from the frustration of manually vendoring gear after every dungeon run. Available on [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/autosellplus) · [Source](https://github.com/mikigraf/AutoSellPlus)

**[CombatXP](https://github.com/mikigraf/CombatXP)** · The Elder Scrolls Online · Lua  
Displays experience and skill points gained during combat directly in your chat window. Simple, lightweight, does one thing well — tells you exactly how much XP that pack of mobs was worth so you can optimize your grind route. Built because ESO's default UI buries this info.
